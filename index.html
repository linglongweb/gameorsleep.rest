About Slice Master

Slice Master is an addictive arcade-style game where your goal is to slice as many fruits as possible while avoiding bombs. The game combines quick reflexes with strategic timing to create an engaging and challenging experience.

As you progress, the game becomes more challenging with faster fruit speeds and special items that can either help or hinder your progress. How many fruits can you slice before the game ends?

Game Controls

Mouse Controls

  • Left Click: Slice fruits
  • Mouse Movement: Control slicing direction

Touch Controls

  • Tap and Drag: Slice fruits
  • Swipe: Create slicing lines
