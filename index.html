About Drift Boss

Drift Boss is an addictive arcade racing game where precision and timing are everything. Navigate challenging tracks by controlling your car's drift angle through sharp turns and obstacles. The longer you drift without crashing, the higher your score! With simple one-touch controls, stunning visuals, and increasingly difficult levels, Drift Boss offers endless hours of fun for racing enthusiasts.

How to Play

Objective: Drift as far as possible without crashing.
Controls: Tap and hold to drift left, release to drift right.
Strategy: Time your drifts perfectly to navigate through sharp turns and obstacles. The longer you maintain your drift without crashing, the higher your score. Collect coins to unlock new cars with better handling and drift capabilities.
