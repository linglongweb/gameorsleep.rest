TANKS OF THE GALAXY

Command your battle tank through alien planets and defeat enemy forces in this action-packed space shooter

Galactic Warfare

In the year 2287, humanity has expanded across the galaxy, but peace is fragile. As commander of the most advanced battle tank in the Terran Alliance, you're deployed to hostile alien planets where you must eliminate enemy forces, protect strategic locations, and uncover ancient alien technologies.

Navigate through treacherous extraterrestrial terrains, from the molten lava fields of Pyro IV to the frozen tundras of Cryos Prime. Each planet presents unique environmental challenges that affect your tank's mobility and combat capabilities.

Tank Customization

Upgrade your tank with over 50 different weapons, armor, and propulsion systems to match your combat style.

Alien Planets

Battle across 12 unique alien worlds with distinct environments, enemies, and tactical challenges.

Enemy Variety

Face over 30 different enemy types from swarming drones to massive alien walkers and boss tanks.

Combat Tactics

Master the art of mechanized warfare with an arsenal of futuristic weapons. Switch between your primary cannon, secondary missiles, and special abilities to counter different enemy types. Use the environment to your advantage - take cover behind alien rock formations, lure enemies into environmental hazards, and position yourself on higher ground for tactical superiority.

Your tank's energy shield absorbs damage but requires strategic management. Overuse will leave you vulnerable during critical cooldown periods. Learn to balance offensive pressure with defensive positioning to survive the increasingly difficult enemy waves.

Progression System

Earn experience points and credits through successful missions. Unlock new tank chassis with specialized abilities - from lightning-fast scout tanks to heavily armored siege engines. Research alien technology to develop powerful hybrid weapons that combine human engineering with extraterrestrial power sources.

Your pilot gains special abilities as they level up, allowing you to temporarily boost your tank's performance, call in orbital strikes, or deploy combat drones. Choose skill paths that complement your preferred combat style - become a mobile skirmisher, an unstoppable juggernaut, or a long-range artillery specialist.

Advanced Strategies

To dominate the galactic battlefields, consider these advanced tactics:

1. Weapon Synergy: Combine weapons that complement each other. Use area-effect weapons to strip shields followed by precision weapons to damage critical components.

2. Terrain Utilization: Different terrains affect mobility. Use hover mode on marshy planets and switch to tracked movement on rocky surfaces.

3. Weak Point Targeting: Study enemy designs to identify and target critical weak points for maximum damage.

4. Energy Management: Balance weapon fire with shield regeneration. Overheating your systems leaves you vulnerable.

5. Adaptive Loadouts: Customize your tank before each mission based on the planet environment and expected enemy types.
